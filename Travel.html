Airports

The two closest Airports are Frankfurt and Frankfurt Hahn.

Frankfurt

- Airlines flying here include Aer Lingus, Ryanair, Lufthansa and British Airways.

- This is the easiest one to travel from as there is a train direct from the airport to Heidelberg.

Train: Deutsche Bahn.

Frankfurt Hahn

- Only Ryanair fly here.

- This is a bit more difficult to travel from but there is one infrequent bus to Heidelberg.

Bus: Hahn Express.
